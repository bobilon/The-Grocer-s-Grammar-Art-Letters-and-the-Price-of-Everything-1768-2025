# Art × Letters × Science × Industry × Education × Media, 1768–2000

### A Brief Introduction Towards a Consideration of the Y2K Analog-to-Digital Migration to the Year 2025

*Bobby Bress — working repository, drafts in progress*

---

## What this is

This is the build site for a long work in progress. The thesis, stated plainly: from the founding of the Royal Academy in 1768 to the Y2K threshold, six strands of English-language civilization — art, letters, science, industry, education, and media — were braided together at their origin in a single printing-and-etching lineage, came apart over two centuries into separate institutions each policing the same class boundary, and then hit, at the millennium, a digital migration that dissolved the physical scarcity their whole apparatus of value depended on. What a manufactured scarcity becomes when the substrate that enforced it goes infinitely reproducible is the question the work takes up. The art market is the worked example. Education is the proof that the boundary was always the point and the object was only ever its enforcement mechanism.

The through-line that lets these be one history rather than six parallel essays is the etched mark: the plate that printed Blake's illuminated books, the plate that printed the newspaper, the plate that became the etched silicon chip. Pictures and processors are the same lineage at two ends of the analog era. The Academy and the fab are the same gatekeeping logic in two media.

These pieces are drafts. They are meant to be read, argued with, and cut. Facts have been checked where they are load-bearing; the harder claims are flagged in-text as claims. Nothing here is a finished position.

## How to read it

The pieces are independent but they sequence. Read in this order for the argument; read any one alone for its own sake.

**1. [Setting the Table — Ireland as the Test Site That Bore the Modern Artist](./setting-the-table-ireland.md)**
The opening. Defines the key term — *the Artist*, as the word comes to mean by Y2K — through the one case where the education boundary was nakedly the colonial boundary, with no native system underneath it. Trinity as conquest-by-university; Newman's locked-out Catholic University that could teach but not confer; the Jesuit rigor meeting the economic taint; and Joyce as the output the pressure forced — the prototype of the Artist as aberrant compulsion, *and* the prototype of the maneuver: the maker who packages his own difficulty through a credentialed intermediary. The key and the counterfeit-key in one figure.

**2. [A General History of English-Language Education, 1768–2000](./general-history-of-education-1768-2000.md)**
The widest view. Both countries, two centuries, the whole board. The recurring machinery: mass literacy manufactured to a purpose (economic, military, the management of the enfranchised); the boundary between the trained hand and the commanding mind, policed through fees and exams and the names of institutions; the name as a class claim and the renaming as a class migration; the boundary reconstituting itself through whatever variable stays unregulated. Royal Academy, Forster, Balfour, Butler; common school, Morrill, the GI Bill, Sputnik, Brown; the institutional climbs of Pitt and Strathclyde. Sources and where the forensic work lives are listed at the end.

**3. [Appendix — The Education Strand](./appendix-education-strand.md)**
The same education history, tightened and pointed at the art thesis — the "school/academy" boundary read as the same split as "print/painting," the made versus the anointed. A bridge document between the general history and the market argument. To be folded in or cut.

**4. [The End of the Grand Game](./the-end-of-the-grand-game.md)**
The theoretical spine. The two-layer frame: the extractive-capitalist vantage as a lens, and the specific market maneuver built on it. The law that technical superiority does not predict captured value. The missing economic variable — expenditure, the accursed share, the thing left out of Marx and quarantined in the literature departments — and the art crash as its empirical proof case. The control cases (Richter, Heizer) that show what honest production looks like. The 1980 freeze as an allocation consequence. The correction that matters most: they looted the wrong medium — the real generational-wealth carry was in the catalogs, evolving, while the capital piled into static decorative objects with manufactured scarcity.

## A note on method

The vantage is the grocer's grammar: pre-theoretical, evidence-based knowledge of what things actually cost, used as the investigative instrument. The claim throughout is not that any single object is a fraud but that an entire class of object was priced on a manufactured scarcity the digital migration is now dissolving — and that the institutions which should have read it in real time did not, because the framework that would have named it had been kept in the wrong building. The facts have owners. The braid is open. That open braid is the contribution.
